6 Quick Easy Steps to Build Your Own Circuit!

Step 1 - Enter how many elements in your circuit:

Currently, the circuit solver can handle a maximum of 20 elements

Step 2 - Drag and drop elements from the table into circuit boxes

Must include one battery in the circuit
Order of the elements does not matter

Step 3 - Enter voltage or resistance values for circuit elements

Extreme values may cause arithmetic error and voltage or current may appear as 0

Step 4 (Optional) - Fill in empty boxes with wire from the table

Step 5 - Press "Submit Circuit" and watch correct voltage and current values appear
